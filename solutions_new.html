Solutions

Solving Your Procurement Challenges

Puridiom solutions are flexible in design and functionality. Modular in design, our solutions are built to solve your procurement problems. Whether you are looking for a total procurement transformation, a requisition and approval workflow tool to integrate with an existing ERP, or a solution to help manage suppliers, Puridiom is your answer. Procurement Simplified. Results Guaranteed.

Workflow Manager

Integrate requisition and approval workflows with existing ERP

The Puridiom Workflow Manager is a front-end requisition and approval workflow solution that easily integrates to your existing ERP. Access to internal catalogs and punch-outs are also included in this tool.

Plan-To-Pay

Total Procurement Transformation from Requisition Planning to Payment

The Plan-to-Pay solution is a modular suite that streamlines and automates procurement processes. From requisition to receipt of invoice, Plan-to-Pay covers it all.
