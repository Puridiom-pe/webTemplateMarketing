Puridiom Defines Procure-to-Pay

Procure-to-Pay encompasses the entire purchasing process from requisition to payment, including purchase orders, receiving, invoice reconciliation, and management of vendors and suppliers and payment. A true Procure-to-Pay solution comprehensively automates all of these processes to make them flow seamlessly while collecting the data necessary for CPO/CFO business planning. This type of solution is recommended for organizations with little or no procurement automation and is looking to transform procurement into an efficient and cost-effective process.

The Puridiom solution is a comprehensive all-in-one procure-to-pay product that has evolved through years of working with procurement professionals. Self-help tools enable users to more efficiently and effectively work through each step of the procurement process. Essential data is continually gathered for reporting and planning purposes. Modular design allows you to select only the pieces of the procurement solution that your organization needs, and allows you to add as you gain functionality.
