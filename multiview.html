Cloud Implementation

Quicker Deployment
Lower Overall Cost

Cloud deployments are quicker to deploy, enable you to stay current with automatic updates, provide independence from IT, improve usability, and lower overall cost of ownership. No hardware or maintenance is required by the organization, including security. Fee is subscription based.

On-Premise Implentation

More Control of Software, Fewer Integration Challenges

On-Premise deployments require more investment up-front, to include initial implementation and license fees, hardware infrastructure acquisition and maintenance costs, administrative and internal resource costs for support. However, if organization already has infrastructure in place and has a need to keep data behind the firewall for security purposed, this deployment may be the way to go. Other benefits include control of software and fewer integration challenges.
