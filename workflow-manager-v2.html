Workflow Manager

Integration and Management

Easily Integrate purchasing processes with any ERP

Looking for an easy way to enter requisitions, manage workflows, or access your catalogs but still maintain the data flow and connection with your ERP? The Puridiom Workflow Manager can help you with all of these process and more.

Requests

Manage any type of request for any product or service.

Approvals

Easily dictate the approval flow to expedite approvals

Budget Control

Keep budgets on track and in check

Standard Catalogs

Access and search all standard catalogs stored history features

Punchout Catalogs

One click access to outside catalogs with search and history features.

Expense Manager

Track expenses before, during and after travel.

Requests

Enter any type of request, send it for approval, and ensure that established rules and best practice strategies are maintained.

  • Simple to complex requisitions
  • All request type templates
  • Shopping cart search and buy features
  • Intelligent Request Engine™
  • Business Validation Rule Engine™

Approvals

Automatically route requests for approval based on your company's hierarchical structure.

  • Faster, compliant approvals
  • View workflow approvers
  • Check status instantly
  • Approve from any device
  • Eliminate backlog

Standard Catalog Management

Standard catalog management provides access to internally managed catalogs.

  • One-step access to internal catalogs
  • Shopping cart experience
  • Keyword searchable
  • Ensured compliance with contracts/pricing

Punchout Catalog Management

Access the supplier's webite directly from Puridiom and search catalog data for required infomration.

  • Transparent connection to suppliers’ catalogs
  • Automatic updates of catalog content
  • Shopping cart experience
  • Search on approved items

Budget Control

Check the availability of funds through each step of the purchasing process.

  • Control spend with budgets
  • Fiscal period and project budget controls
  • Automatic checks for all process stages
  • Audit trail

Expense Manager

Capture expense details on your mobile device, including the images of receipts.

  • Record and track expenses
  • Manage receipts and expense details
  • Automatically submit for approval & payment processing
