About Puridiom
About our Partners
Contact Us

Puridiom has built strong partner relationships with well known industry vendors in order to provide customized solutions for our clients. We utilize these partnerships to streamline processes and ensure the lowest cost, highest quality services across all platforms.

Puridiom Partners take advantage of the leadership we command in the Enterprise Procurement software industry. The reciprocal partnerships we build bring a wealth of value and knowledge to our mutual clients. We invite you to contact us to learn more about becoming a Puridiom Partner.

Our Strategic Alliances:

Aegis is a global business services provider in the area of experience management. They provide a suite of solutions for our clients, from strategy and design to implementation and execution, that help global brands deliver memorable end-customer experiences. With a presence in 56 locations across 13 countries and with more than 55,000 employees, Aegis manages over a billion customer interactions every year for over 300 clients across verticals such as BFSI, Telecom, Healthcare, Travel and Hospitality, Consumer Goods, Retail, and Technology.

http://www.aegisglobal.com/

ATRIIS provides industry leading, on-demand travel spend management solutions for corporate business travel programs. Our services include spend analytics and sourcing, contract management, e-procurement, approvals, policy compliance, supplier rationalization and collaboration and strategic planning.

www.atriis.com

Informatica Corporation (Nasdaq: INFA) is the world’s number one independent provider of data integration software. Organizations around the world rely on Informatica to realize their information potential and drive top business imperatives. Informatica Vibe, the industry’s first and only embeddable virtual data machine (VDM), powers the unique “Map Once. Deploy Anywhere.” capabilities of the Informatica Platform. Worldwide, over 5,000 enterprises depend on Informatica to fully leverage their information assets from devices to mobile to social to big data residing on-premise, in the Cloud and across social networks.

http://www.informatica.com/us/company/about-informatica/

Office Depot is an $11 billion+ global supplier of office products and services and is a core member of the Puridiom Marketplace. Through the Puridiom Marketplace, Office Depot offers Puridiom customers substantial discounts on office supplies, print and mail services, technology products, janitorial and breakroom supplies, furniture and customer branded promotional products, fund raising, and company store merchandise.

http://www.officedepot.com/

Fanatical Support has made Rackspace the service leader in cloud computing. They deliver enterprise-level hosting services to businesses of all sizes and kinds around the world.

www.rackspace.com

SunGard iWORKS is a business-driven IT product family for the insurance industry in each of the following major business lines: life/health/annuities/pensions, property and casualty and reinsurance. iWORKS offers a range of products and services including front-office tools, policy administration, reinsurance, actuarial calculations, financial and investment accounting and reporting.

www.sungardinsurance.com

Incorporated in 1997, Thakral One bridges the gap between business and information technology in emerging markets. It provides robust IT infrastructure solutions and services, implements Puridiom's enterprise application, and offers long-term managed support to Puridiom clients. Thakral One is headquarted in Singapore and is present in 15 countries across Asia.

http://www.thakralone.com/

WEX today provides category-leading fleet, corporate social and prepaid payment solutions across a wide spectrum of business sectors around the globe and is a top-ten issuer of commercial payment purchasing soltions in the U.S. and the industry pacesetter in single-use virtual account and AP solutions. We proudly issue Mastercard® branded credit products through WEX Bank, our wholly owned industrial bank.

http://www.wexinc.com/
