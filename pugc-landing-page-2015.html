Airports

Harrisburg International Airport is approximately 15 minutes from Hershey, PA. The airport is accessible to the region’s major interstates and highways via a 4-lane limited access highway. Seven major airlines operate out of HIA with over 120 daily flights to and from Harrisburg for your convenience, including direct flights from most major Eastern cities.

Shuttle Service

Hershey Lodge offers shuttle service to and from the Harrisburg International Airport for a minimal fee. We recommend you make arrangements with the Hershey Lodge Concierge for airport pick-up in advance of your arrival. Contact them directly by calling 717-534-8601.

Vehicle Rental

Four national rental car companies are located on-site at HIA. Three operate from off-site locations served by a shuttle bus. An Enterprise-Rent-A-Car counter is located at Hershey Lodge in the front lobby.
