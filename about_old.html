Our Mission

Puridiom's quest to perfect the science of procurement has come a long way. Today, we can help you with your procurement processes anywhere, any time.

UNPARALLELED SERVICES

In 1983, Technical Services Associates Inc. (TSA), a small software development company specializing in procurement, set the stage by changing how procurement processes could be made more efficient by introducing automation of core procurement functions on a personal computer. Even though TSA was small in size, our innovative ideas, creativity, and domain expertise allowed us to lay the foundation for e-Procurement.
